Mortgage Requirements

Property Security

All mortgages must be secured by a property, which of course can be the property you are buying (assuming it is in private ownership).

Repayment Timeline

Most mortgages must be repaid by your 70th birthday (some banks may allow 5 more years), so the maximum length is dependent on your age.

Loan-to-Value (LTV)

The maximum Loan-to-Value (LTV) is dependent on the value of the property in question and also your status. Interest rate is affected by the LTV in addition to your status.
