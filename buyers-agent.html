Buyer's Agent Services

Professional assistance in finding and acquiring the perfect property for your needs and budget in Czech Republic.

Service Overview

Property Search Services

  • Property search: Help finding properties that match your criteria
  • Attend viewings: Translation and expert guidance on what to look for
  • Reporting: A full report after each viewing detailing what was discussed and some figures
  • Negotiation: Negotiating the price and contract conditions of your selected property
  • Closing: Helping you right through the closing process, including switching over utility suppliers and help with the tax payment.

Service Packages

Buyers Agent Lite

Our basic package is ideal for those who already have a property selected, but have concerns. Is the agency reputable? Is the price OK? Have we missed any obvious flaws?

Price: 25,000 CZK (fixed fee)

Includes:

  • 2 viewings
  • 2 detailed reports
  • Price and contract negotiation
  • Complete closing process support
  • Key handover and land registry registration

Buyers Agent (Full Package)

Our full package is ideal for those who are starting their journey to buying a property. We will source properties to your specifications, organise and attend viewings, provide reporting, negotiate and help you through the closing process, through to handing over the keys and registration in the land registry.

Pricing:

  • We try to pass our fee to the seller's agent (80% success rate)
  • If not possible: 1% of purchase price
  • For purchases over 10 million: Contact for individual offer
